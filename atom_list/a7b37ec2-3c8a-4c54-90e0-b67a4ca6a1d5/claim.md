### Claim
DeepSeek-V3's MTP implementation sequentially predicts $D$ additional tokens (D=1 for V3), keeping the complete causal chain at each prediction depth. Each MTP module shares embedding and output head with the main model, has a dedicated Transformer block $TRM_k$ and projection $M_k$. The MTP loss $\mathcal{L}_{MTP}$ is weighted by $\lambda$ (0.3 for first 10T tokens, 0.1 for remaining 4.8T). Ablation shows MTP consistently improves benchmarks (Small MoE: MMLU 53.3 vs 50.0, HumanEval 26.8 vs 20.7). MTP modules can be discarded at inference or repurposed for speculative decoding (85-90% acceptance rate, 1.8x TPS).
